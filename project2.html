Ez-Seat Restaurant Booking App High-Fidelity Wireframe



This is a high-fidelity wireframe that I have designed for a restaurant booking app called EZ-Seat using Figma. This app mainly allows users to place their seat reservations at their preferred restaurants at discounted rates with ease. They can also browse through restaurants which are currently popular and near their location.

Features

  • Easy seat reservations at preferred restaurants
  • Discounted rates for reservations
  • Browse popular and nearby restaurants
  • User-friendly interface

Technologies Used

  • Figma for wireframing
  • Miro for designing the sitemap

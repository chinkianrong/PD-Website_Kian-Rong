VR Escape Room



This image shows one of the rooms I have designed for my Escape Room project, after applying visual effects to the scene. The gameplay for this room is simple: the player walks around the room to search for a hidden key. Then, he/she walks to the door with that key to open it.

Features

  • A basic escape room game with puzzles to go through rooms
  • Puzzles to test the player's ability to solve challenges

Technologies Used

  • Unity for building the escape room scene and programming the gameplay
